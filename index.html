Roger Smith

Lengendary Hunter

Roger was born on August 11, 1958 in Burnips, MI. He loved to go hunting and fishing with his father Arden Smith. He graduated from Hamilton High School in 1976.


He worked most of his professional life at Hart and Cooley in Holland, MI. Roger had three children Nathaniel, Sonya, and Eric. He loved his children and enjoyed watching them play sports and taking them hunting and fishing like his father did.


Roger was a talented athlete playing softball for many years. He started his own business called Roger's Sport Shop in celebration of his love of the outdoors. He spent most of his free time enjoying the many outdoor activities he loved all of his life. He passed away in May of 2012.
